## Summary

这段内容是在介绍 Kendall Jenner 的山间住宅设计风格，以及客厅、厨房、餐厅、卧室和浴室的布置思路。整段表达很口语化，适合用来学习“描述房间”“表达审美偏好”“介绍设计想法”这类英文说法。

## Useful Vocabulary

| Word / Phrase | Meaning | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| communal | 公共的，适合大家一起使用的 | 常用于描述空间或氛围 |
| cozy | 舒适温暖的 | 口语里非常常见 |
| whimsical | 富有趣味的，带一点奇思妙想的 | 常用于描述风格 |
| neutral | 中性的，柔和不过分抢眼的 | 常用于配色和设计 |
| statement piece | 很吸睛的重点单品 | 常用于穿搭或家居表达 |

## Useful Expressions

- I wanted it to feel a little bit more neutral.
- It turned out perfectly.
- I wanted to test my limits.
- It feels really fun and refreshing.
- This room gets a lot of action.

## Review Notes

- `turn out` 很常用，表示“结果变成……；最后效果是……”。  
- `I wanted it to feel...` 是表达设计目标、个人偏好的自然句型。  
- `a little bit more + adjective` 是很柔和的比较表达，口语里特别常见。  
- 描述房间和空间时，可以积累这些形容词：`cozy`, `communal`, `neutral`, `whimsical`。  
- `statement piece` 值得重点记住，适合以后描述衣服、家具、装饰品。  

## Clean Transcript

Hey AD, it's me again. Welcome to my mountain house.

We are in the living room now. I worked with Heidi Kier on this house, and I really wanted a kind of grandma chic feeling, with lots of patterns, fabrics, and textures.

This house feels very experimental for me. I wanted to test my limits and see how far we could take the design while still making it feel like me.

This is the most communal room in the house. We watch movies here, people gather here, and it makes me really happy when the space is full.

I wanted the kitchen to feel open and connected to the family room. In the end, I decided to keep it a little more neutral because the rest of the house already had so much going on.

The dining room is meant to feel like a sun room. The light comes in beautifully, and it has become an important place for dinners and conversations.

The bar is one of the most important rooms in the house. I had a lot of fun collecting western art and adding personality to the space.

I wanted the powder room to feel charming and cute. It is small, but it has a lot of personality.

The bedroom feels both mature and childlike at the same time. I wanted it to be a room where you would want to sit down and read a million books.

The bathrooms are one of the highlights of the house. I especially love the color palettes and the playful design choices.
