## Grammar Review

### 1) Recent event needs past tense

- Wrong: `The shower in my apartment just stop working.`
- Correct: `The shower in my apartment just stopped working.`
- Note: `just + 过去发生的动作` 常用过去式表达“刚刚发生”。

### 2) Passive voice form

- Wrong: `You will be remove from the building.`
- Correct: `You will be removed from the building.`
- Note: 被动语态结构是 `be + 过去分词`。

### 3) Fixed phrase: in front of

- Wrong: `In the front of us.`
- Correct: `In front of us.`
- Note: 表示“在我们前面（外部）”固定说法是 `in front of`。

### 4) Adverb form after remember

- Wrong: `If I remember correct.`
- Correct: `If I remember correctly.`
- Note: 这里需要副词 `correctly` 修饰“记得是否正确”。

### 5) Repair-reporting sentence patterns

- Less natural: `My shower in my apartment is stopped.`
- Better: `My apartment shower is not working.` / `The shower in my apartment isn't working.`
- Note: 设备故障常用 `isn't working`，不用 `is stopped`。

### 6) Food-serving context

- Confusing: `There we go, your hamburger.`
- Natural: `Here we go, your burger.`
- Note: 递给顾客东西时 `Here we go` 更贴场景。

## Pronunciation Tips

- `apartment`: 美式口语里 `t` 常弱化，连读时听起来接近 `apar'ment`。
- `license` vs `lines`: `license` 读 /ˈlaɪsəns/，尾部是 `-səns`，不是 `lines`。
- `correctly`: /kəˈrektli/，重音在 `-rect-`。
- `Dragon Boat Festival`: 中间的 `boat` 不能省略。
- `Austria` /ˈɒstriə/ vs `Australia` /ɒˈstreɪliə/：后者音节更长、重音位置不同。
