## Grammar Review

### 1) `where` 疑问句要倒装

- Wrong: `Where she it is.`
- Correct: `Where is she?` / `Where is it?`
- Note: 用 `where` 提问时，`be` 动词通常放在主语前面。

### 2) `stuck` 前面要有 `be` 或 `get`

- Wrong: `I stuck in traffic.`
- Correct: `I got stuck in traffic.` / `I was stuck in traffic.`
- Note: `stuck` 在这里是形容词，不能单独当谓语用。

### 3) 红绿灯前通常用 `at`

- Wrong: `I stuck in traffic light.` / `I got stuck in the traffic light.`
- Correct: `I got stuck at the traffic light.` / `I'm stuck at the light.`
- Note: 说“卡在红绿灯那里”时用 `at the light`，不是 `in the traffic light`。

### 4) `want` 后面接 `to + 动词`

- Wrong: `Come with me if you want leave.`
- Correct: `Come with me if you want to leave.` / `Come with me if you want to live.`
- Note: `want` 后面用不定式；同时注意 `leave` 和 `live` 不是一个词。

### 5) 单数可数名词前通常需要冠词

- Wrong: `Get dog.`
- Correct: `Get a dog.`
- Note: `dog` 是单数可数名词，通常要加 `a` / `the` 或其他限定词。

### 6) 完整句子需要主语

- Wrong: `Are the bodyguards.` / `Are the bad guys.`
- Correct: `They are the bodyguards.` / `They are the bad guys.`
- Note: 英语陈述句通常不能省掉主语。

### 7) “换钱”说 `exchange money`

- Wrong: `I'm going to shift money.`
- Correct: `I'm going to exchange some money.`
- Note: `shift` 是“移动”，不表示兑换货币。

### 8) 正在进行更自然地说 `in progress`

- Less natural: `Passenger announcement is in process.`
- Better: `A passenger announcement is in progress.` / `A passenger announcement is being made.`
- Note: 描述“正在进行中”时，`in progress` 比 `in process` 更自然。

### 9) `standard of living` 比 `living standard` 更地道

- Acceptable: `living standard`
- More natural: `standard of living`
- Example: `Their standard of living is still a bit higher than that of ordinary Chinese families.`
- Note: 两种都能懂，但完整表达里本族语者更常说 `standard of living`。

### 10) 现代口语里常直接把 `livestream` 当动词

- Fine: `find a good family to do live streaming with`
- More natural: `find a good family to livestream with`
- Note: 现在 `livestream` 常直接作动词使用，表达更紧凑。

## Pronunciation Tips

- `radiation`: /ˌreɪdiˈeɪʃn/，重音在 `-AY-`，可以按 `ray-dee-AY-shən` 去练。
- `altitude`: /ˈæl.təˌtuːd/，重音在第一音节，不要把重音放到后面。
- `cashier`: /kæˈʃɪr/，第二音节更重，结尾像 `sheer`。
- `luggage`: /ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/，开头像 `luck`，不要读成多音节的拼写读法。
- `pictures`: /ˈpɪktʃərz/，中间常听起来像 `pik-chers`。
- `live` /lɪv/ 和 `leave` /liːv/ 要分开练：前者短音，后者长音。
