## 🧠 语法复盘 (Grammar Review)

### 1) 表达“很困”
- ❌ I want to sleep.（只表达“想去睡觉”的意愿）
- ✅ I’m **sleepy**. / I’m **so sleepy**.
- 说明：**sleepy** = 困的（状态）；**want to sleep** = 想去睡觉（意愿）。

### 2) gonna 用法
- ❌ I’m gonna **to** sleeping.
- ✅ I’m gonna **sleep**.
- 说明：**gonna = going to**，后面直接接动词原形，不加 to，不用 -ing。

### 3) to + 动词原形
- ❌ I’m going to **sleeping** now.
- ✅ I’m going to **sleep** now.
- 说明：to 后面永远接动词原形。

### 4) -ing 的正确场景
- ✅ I’m tired **from sleeping** late.（介词后用 -ing）
- ✅ a **sleeping** baby / He is **sleeping** now.（作定语 / 进行时）

### 5) 现在完成时 vs 一般过去时
- ❌ I **have go** to the company to make some meeting.
- ✅ I **went** to the company to **have a meeting**.
- ✅ I **have gone** to the company to report on my work.（强调“已去/刚去”）
- 说明：**have/has + 过去分词**；meeting 用 **have/attend**。

### 6) report vs reporter
- ❌ I have gone to **reporter** my work.
- ✅ I have gone to **report** (on) my work.
- 说明：**reporter** = 记者；**report** = 汇报。

### 7) 年龄表达
- ❌ in my 28 years old
- ✅ **at the age of 28** / **when I was 28**

### 8) is + to do 结构
- ❌ The difficult thing **is make** my partner trust me.
- ✅ The difficult thing **is to make** my partner trust me.

### 9) 名词/动词混用
- ❌ continue to **success**
- ✅ continue to **succeed** / keep **succeeding**

### 10) 主谓一致 & 情态动词
- ❌ he **have** earned / will **told**
- ✅ he **has** earned / will **tell**

### 11) 词义区分：competitor vs colleague
- ❌ college / companioner（误用）
- ✅ **competitor** = 竞争对手
- ✅ **colleague** = 同事

### 12) influence 表达
- ❌ I can’t influence me.
- ✅ It doesn’t influence me. / It has no influence on me.

### 13) 价格比较
- ❌ I no be one cent less than the companion.
- ✅ Our price is **one cent lower than** the competitor’s.

### 14) settle vs settlement
- ❌ upload the settle / settlement settlement
- ✅ upload the **settlement** / settlement document

## 🗣️ 发音强化 (Pronunciation Tips)

- **competitor** /kəmˈpetɪtər/：重音在 -pet-。
- **colleague** /ˈkɑːliːɡ/：结尾是 /liːɡ/，不是 /lɪdʒ/。
- **criticized** /ˈkrɪtɪˌsaɪzd/：结尾 /-saɪzd/ 清晰。
- **conversation** /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪʃən/：重音在 -sa-。
- **forecast** /ˈfɔːrˌkæst/；**calculate** /ˈkælkjəˌleɪt/：注意 /kæl/ 起音。
- **influence** /ˈɪnfluəns/：三音节，/flu/ 清晰。
- **schedule** /ˈskedʒuːl/（美式）：首音节重读。
- **settlement** /ˈsɛtəlmənt/：/təl/ 轻读。
