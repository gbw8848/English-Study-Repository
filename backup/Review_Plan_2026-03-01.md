## Grammar Review

### 1) 金额口语读法

- Context: `thirty-two twenty-five`
- Meaning in money context: `$32.25`
- Full form: `thirty-two dollars and twenty-five cents`
- Note: 在价格场景里，省略 `dollars/cents` 很常见。

### 2) `date` 不是 `data`

- Wrong: `OK, you can data her.`
- Correct: `OK, you can date her.`
- Note: `date` 是“约会”；`data` 是“数据”。

### 3) `condo` vs `candle`（语义要对场景）

- Wrong (if gift is a candle): `My mom got me this condo as a gift for moving here.`
- Correct: `My mom got me this candle as a gift for moving here.`
- Note: `condo` 是房子，`candle` 是蜡烛。

### 4) `kind of + 名词`

- Wrong: `My mom got me this kind I'd like a gift for moving here.`
- Better: `My mom got me this kind of candle as a gift for moving here.`
- Note: `kind of` 后面要直接接名词。

### 5) `obsessed` 常见结构

- Wrong: `I get an obsessed with it.`
- Correct: `I got obsessed with it.`
- Also natural: `I'm obsessed with it.`

### 6) 名词单数结构

- Wrong: `Look how beautiful this are arrangement is.`
- Correct: `Look how beautiful this arrangement is.`
- Note: `this + 单数名词`，不需要 `are`。

### 7) `flashlight` 是一个词

- Better: `It's flashlight time.`
- Note: 口语里会听到连读，但拼写是 `flashlight`（一个单词）。

## Pronunciation Tips

- `condo` /ˈkɑːndoʊ/ vs `candle` /ˈkændl/: 中间元音不同，结尾也不同（`-doʊ` vs `-dl`）。
- `peanut butter` /ˈpiːnʌt ˌbʌtər/: `peanut` 第一音节重读，`butter` 美音常有轻微闪音。
- `flashlight` /ˈflæʃlaɪt/: 重音在 `flash`，后半部分要清楚读出 `/laɪt/`。
- `arrangement` /əˈreɪndʒmənt/: 重音在第二音节 `-range-`。
- `Secaucus, New Jersey`: `Secaucus` 常读 /sɪˈkɔːkəs/，注意中间重音。
