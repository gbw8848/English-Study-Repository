## Grammar Review

### 1) `should` 后接动词原形

- Wrong: `You should to go there one time.`
- Correct: `You should go there once.`
- Note: `should` 后面不能再加 `to`，`once` 比 `one time` 更自然。

### 2) `gonna` 结构

- Wrong: `I gonna to have a great time.`
- Correct: `I'm gonna have a great time.`
- Correct: `I'm going to have a great time.`
- Note: `gonna` 已经等于 `going to`，不能重复 `to`。

### 3) `go + 动名词` 固定搭配

- Wrong: `We are gonna to camping and bushwalking.`
- Correct: `We're gonna go camping and bushwalking.`
- Note: 说“去做某活动”常用 `go + V-ing`。

### 4) 进行时：`be + V-ing`

- Wrong: `Why are you giggle?`
- Correct: `Why are you giggling?`

### 5) 过去时间状语要用过去式

- Wrong: `I delete the apps 10 minutes ago.`
- Correct: `I deleted the apps 10 minutes ago.`
- Wrong: `I grab my phone.`
- Correct: `I grabbed my phone.`

### 6) 常见搭配和介词

- Wrong: `I guess I will just go work.`
- Correct: `I guess I'll just go to work.`
- Wrong: `You know, I'm running on the park.`
- Correct: `You know, I'm running in the park.`
- Wrong: `Where is the trail is?`
- Correct: `Where is the trail?`

### 7) 一段时间：`for + 时间`

- Wrong: `I have been hiking 14 hours.`
- Correct: `I have been hiking for 14 hours.`

### 8) 动词选择

- Wrong: `I already done my coffee.`
- Correct: `I already drank my coffee.`
- Also natural: `I already downed my coffee.` (very casual)

### 9) 拼写与发音对应

- Wrong: `Who was so talenten?`
- Correct: `Who was so talented?`

## Pronunciation Tips

- `Steve` /stiːv/ vs `Steven` /ˈstiːvən/: `Steven` 末尾多一个弱读 `-ən`。
- `trigger warning`: 重点读对 `trigger`，避免听成 `trick/drag warring`。
- `giggling` /ˈɡɪɡlɪŋ/: 双 `g` 后接 `-ing`，中间不丢音。
- `talented` /ˈtæləntɪd/: 结尾是 `-tɪd`，不是 `-ten`。
- `once` /wʌns/: 单词短促，尾音是 `-ns`。
