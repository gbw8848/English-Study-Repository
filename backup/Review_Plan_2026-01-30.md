## 🔎 语法复盘 (Grammar Review)

### 1) “销售岗位”表达
- ❌ I’m sales.
- ✅ I’m **in sales**.
- ✅ I work **in sales**.

### 2) like 后接动名词 / 直接问“吸烟”
- ❌ Do you like to smoking?
- ✅ Do you smoke?
- ✅ Do you like smoking?

### 3) just + 过去式
- ❌ I just see the outdoor scenery.
- ✅ I just **saw** the outdoor scenery.

### 4) right away vs right now
- ✅ I went to the hospital **right away**.（过去“立刻”）
- ✅ I need to go to the hospital **right now**.（现在“立刻”）

### 5) 现在完成时：Have you installed...?
- ❌ Do you have installed it on your phone?
- ✅ Have you installed it on your phone?
- ✅ Did you install it on your phone?（问过去）

### 6) “Most of Chinese” → “Most Chinese”
- ❌ Most **of** Chinese can’t speak English.
- ✅ **Most Chinese** can’t speak English.

### 7) foreigners / a foreigner who **speaks**
- ❌ It’s hard to find a foreigner people.
- ✅ It’s hard to find **foreigners**.
- ✅ It’s hard to find a foreigner who **speaks** English.

### 8) 车站介词：at this station
- ❌ Are you getting off **in** this station?
- ✅ Are you getting off **at** this station?

### 9) “停留3分钟”的表达
- ❌ This train only stop for 3 minutes.
- ✅ This train only **stops** for 3 minutes.

### 10) 下车表达：get off / get back on
- ❌ pick up the train
- ✅ get back on (the train)

### 11) “等我一下”固定搭配
- ❌ Wait me a second.
- ✅ Wait **for** me a second.
- ✅ Give me a sec. / Hold on a second.

### 12) 指路：turn left **at** + 距离
- ❌ Turn left 100 meters.
- ✅ Turn left **at** about 100 meters.
- ✅ Go 100 meters, then turn left **at** the 100‑meter mark.

### 13) 语义更自然的替换
- ✅ I’m looking for a chance to practice with a **fluent English speaker**.
- ✅ If customers **run into issues**, we’ll **look into** it.

### 14) 用词得体提醒（避免不当指向）
- ✅ 表达商业运营建议：The platform is better **run by** Company X.（聚焦公司/团队）
- ✅ 说明群体时：**Jewish people**（比 “Jews” 更中性、礼貌）

## 🎧 发音强化 (Pronunciation Tips)

- **sales** /seɪlz/：结尾 /lz/ 连读，不要断开。
- **salesperson** /ˈseɪlzˌpɜːrsən/：重音在第一音节。
- **get off / get back on** /ɡet ɔːf/ ; /ɡet bæk ɑːn/：连读更自然。
- **right away** /raɪt əˈweɪ/：中间弱读 /ə/。
- **at this station** /æt ðɪs ˈsteɪʃn/：/tð/ 连读别吞音。
- **hypocrites** /ˈhɪpəkrɪts/：重音在首音节，结尾 /-ts/。
- **cruel** /krʊəl/：两音节 /kru-əl/，别读成 /krul/。
- **gigawatt** /ˈɡɪɡəwɑːt/：重音在第一个音节。
- **latte** /ˈlæti/：/læ/ 起音，结尾 /-ti/。
- **roasted lamb** /ˈroʊstɪd læm/：roast /roʊst/ 不要漏 /t/。
- **earring** /ˈɪrɪŋ/：注意 /ɪr/ 发音。
- **enchanted** /ɪnˈtʃæntɪd/：/tʃæn/ 清晰。
