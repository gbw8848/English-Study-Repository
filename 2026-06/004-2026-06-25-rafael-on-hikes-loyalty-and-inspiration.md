## Review Notes

- `What do you like to do for fun?` 比 `What are your hobbies?` 更口语、更自然。
- `Did a 100 mile trek` 是口语省略，完整句可以是 `I did a 100-mile trek`。
- `green flags within yourself` 问的是“你自己有哪些好的关系特质”，不是问对方。
- `if you don't teach me something or you don't inspire me` 很适合练习择偶标准表达：强调精神刺激和成长感。
