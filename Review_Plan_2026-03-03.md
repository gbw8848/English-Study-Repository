## Grammar Review

### 1) Pointing at a specific building

- Wrong: `Is that building?`
- Correct: `Is that the building?`
- Note: 指着“那个特定建筑”时要加 `the`。

### 2) Countable noun needs an article

- Wrong: `You are monster.`
- Correct: `You are a monster.`
- Note: `monster` 是可数名词，单数前要有 `a/an`。

### 3) Ask about time with `when` (not `which`)

- Wrong: `I don't know which the last time I have cutted my hair.`
- Correct: `I can't remember when I last cut my hair.`
- Note: 用 `when` 问时间；`cut` 的过去式也是 `cut`，不是 `cutted`。

### 4) More natural city expression

- Good: `You can look around in Shanghai.`
- More natural: `You can look around Shanghai.`
- Note: 口语里 `look around + 城市` 常省略 `in`。

### 5) Natural flashlight expression

- Less natural: `It's flash light time.`
- Better: `Time for the flashlight.` / `It's time to use the flashlight.`
- Note: `flashlight` 是一个词，不分开写。

## Pronunciation Tips

- `Digital Ocean`: 重音在 `DI-gital` 和 `O-cean`，避免读成 `did-you-get ocean`。
- `words` /wɝːdz/ vs `woods` /wʊdz/: 元音不同，结尾辅音群也不同。
- `Iran`: 美式常读 /ɪˈræn/，注意第二音节重读。
- `persistent memory`: 连读时 `persistent` 尾音和 `memory` 会自然衔接。
- `Is that the building?`: `that the` 在快读中会弱读连在一起。
