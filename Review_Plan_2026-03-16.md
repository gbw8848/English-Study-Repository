## Grammar Review

### 1) should not + base verb works well for suggestions and strategy decisions

- Correct: We should not redesign every SEO page from scratch.
- Natural contraction: We shouldn't redesign every SEO page from scratch.
- Softer business alternative: We don't need to redesign every SEO page from scratch.
- Note: use should not when you want to give advice; use don't need to when you want to stress that something is unnecessary.
